Chunky Mango Jam

This was the first thing I made at ISB on the first free weekend we had. It felt very calming and comforting to take some time out to cook in the middle of all the chaos.

Author: Divyashree Salvi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chopped ripe mango
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

(The amount of sugar can be adjusted based on taste and the sweetness of the mango)

Steps

Mix the chopped mango and sugar in a saucepan.

Cook the mixture for around 20 minutes on low-to-medium heat. Stir occasionally as the mango starts to break down. If you prefer a smoother texture, mash the mango as it softens.

Add lemon juice, mix, and heat for another 5 minutes.

Cool the jam before transferring to a sterilised jar. It will thicken a little over the next day.

The jam lasts for around 3 weeks in the fridge.
