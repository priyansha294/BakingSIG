Chicken Tacos

First time 4 of us cooked together.

Author: Prachi Ghalsasi

Ingredients

Meat

  • 500g Chicken
  • Lemon
  • Red chilli powder
  • Peri peri mix
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Green chillies

Salad

  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Coriander
  • Corn
  • Jalape√±os
  • Charred pineapples
  • Lemon
  • Pepper
  • Smoked tobacco sauce

Tortilla

  • Corn flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

1. Mix all ingredients for the meat and let it marinate.

2. Mix the ingredients for the dough and form a stiff dough. Let it rest.

3. Mix all ingredients for the salad in a bowl.

4. Grill the marinated chicken until cooked and juicy.

5. Roll out the dough to form tortillas and grill them.

6. Add cheese and grill the tortillas for 2 minutes.

7. Assemble with the grilled meat and fresh salad. Roll and enjoy!
