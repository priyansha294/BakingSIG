Marble Cake

Cakes sold during the 2nd Bandhan bake sale.

Author: Priyansha Ranjan

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 100g butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 100g condensed milk
  • Vanilla essence
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened chocolate powder

Steps

1. Whip together butter and sugar until soft peaks form. Add the condensed milk and mix with a whisk.

2. Sift together the dry ingredients (except chocolate powder) and gradually add to the wet mixture, alternating with milk. Mix until smooth and lump-free. Add vanilla essence.

3. Divide the batter into two parts. Add the chocolate powder to one part and adjust consistency with a little extra milk.

4. In a greased cake tin, alternate 2 spoons of each batter to create a layered effect. Swirl with a toothpick to make marble designs.

5. Bake at 170Â°C for 40 minutes or until a skewer inserted comes out clean.
