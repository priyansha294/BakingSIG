Ice Cream French Toast

This is a super easy decadent dish. I have made this a few times when I was craving a rich Sunday breakfast and didn't want to burn a hole in my pocket.

Author: Divyashree Salvi

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of milk bread
  • 2 tbsps of butter
  • 200g vanilla ice cream, melted

Steps

1. Pour the melted vanilla ice cream into a shallow bowl. Dip both sides of each bread slice until well coated but not soggy.

2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat. Carefully place the soaked bread onto the pan.

3. Cook each side until golden brown and crisp.

Tip: You can top it off with whatever you love! Try melted chocolate, strawberries, apple cinnamon, or classic pancake syrup.
