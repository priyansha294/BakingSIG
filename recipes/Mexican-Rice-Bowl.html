Mexican Rice Bowl

Tarang Khurana and I sold 100+ plates of this dish to bag the runner's up trophy during Spark 2024.

Author: Drona Nawal

Ingredients

Mexican Rice

  • 1 tbsp Oil + 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1/3 cup Onions (finely chopped)
  • 1/3 cup Tomato puree
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin powder
  • 2 tsp Oregano
  • 2 tsp Red chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp Ketchup
  • 1/2 cup Cooked red rajma beans
  • 1/3 cup Sweet corn
  • 1/3 cup Yellow bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1/3 cup Green bell pepper/capsicum (chopped)
  • 1/3 cup Red bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Fresh jalapeno (sliced)
  • Pinch of Black pepper powder
  • 3 cups Boiled rice
  • 1 tbsp Fresh coriander (finely chopped)

Ananas Pico de Gallo

  • 1 cup Pineapple (chopped)
  • 2/3 cup Onions (chopped)
  • 2 cups Tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Garlic (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Jalapenos (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Coriander (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Queso

  • 1 tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Unsalted butter
  • 3 tbsp Onions (finely diced)
  • 1 tbsp Jalapenos (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp All-purpose flour
  • 5 cups Grated processed cheese
  • 1 cup Whole milk
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Guacamole

  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Jalapenos (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Garlic (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Coriander (chopped)
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 3 Avocados
  • Salt to taste

Steps

Mexican Rice

Set a wok on medium heat, add oil & butter, let the butter melt and turn the flame to high heat. Add garlic and cook for a minute.

Add onion. Stir and cook until the onions are light golden brown.

Add tomato puree & salt, stir and cook for a minute.

Lower the flame and add the powdered spices & tomato ketchup, stir and cook well on high flame.

Add cooked rajma beans, veggies & black pepper powder, gently stir and cook for a minute. Add a splash of water if needed and cook for 2â€“3 minutes.

Add boiled rice and mix gently. Finish with fresh coriander.

Ananas Pico de Gallo

Mix chopped pineapple, onion, tomato and jalapenos in a bowl.

Add salt, lemon juice, red chili powder and coriander. Mix well.

Queso

Heat butter in a medium saucepan. Add onions, jalapenos, garlic and tomato. Season with salt and pepper and cook till soft.

Add flour and stir for 30 seconds, then whisk in milk to form a bechamel.

Whisk in grated cheese a little at a time until fully melted and smooth. Adjust seasoning if needed.

Guacamole

Mash avocados with chopped tomatoes, onions, jalapenos, coriander, and garlic.

Add lemon juice and mix well. Cover and refrigerate to prevent browning.

Plating

Add a serving of Mexican rice to a bowl.

Top with generous spoonfuls of pico de gallo, queso, guacamole, and sour cream.

Add a handful of corn nachos. Garnish with coriander and a wedge of lime.
