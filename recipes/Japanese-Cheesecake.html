Japanese Cheesecake

Made during the Baking SIG Christmas party. Got a kiss for making this.

Author: Priyansha Ranjan

Ingredients

  • 200g cream cheese
  • 5 eggs (whites and yolks separated)
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • Vanilla essence

Steps

1. Mix cream cheese (at room temperature), butter, and 1/4 cup of the sugar using an electric whisk.

2. Add the egg yolks and whisk gently. Then add the corn starch and flour, mixing to ensure there are no clumps.

3. Mix in the milk and a few drops of vanilla essence.

4. In a separate bowl, whip the egg whites with an electric whisk for 30 seconds until frothy. Add the lemon juice and gradually add the remaining sugar. Whip until stiff peaks form.

5. Gently fold a small amount of the meringue into the yolk mixture to lighten it. Then pour this mixture back into the remaining meringue and fold gently. Do not overmix — you want the batter to stay airy.

6. Pour the batter into a cake tin and place the tin in a water bath.

7. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes, then lower the temperature to 140°C and bake for another 20 minutes.

8. Turn off the oven and let the cake rest inside for 10 more minutes before removing.
