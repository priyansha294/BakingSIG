The Greatest Cookie

I had a fantastic time baking cookies with the Baking SIG during Bandhan at Goel's Kitchen. It was a delightful experience collaborating with fellow culinary enthusiasts to raise funds for the extraordinary fest.

Author: Drona Nawal

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups (230g) cake flour
  • 2 cups (275g) all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 tsp (5g) kosher salt
  • 2 tsp (8g) cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp (6g) baking soda
  • 1.25 cups (280g) unsalted butter
  • 1.25 cups (285g) brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115g) white granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 165g walnuts
  • 450g chopped chocolate

Steps

1. In a medium saucepan, melt the unsalted butter and set aside to cool.

2. Roughly chop the dark chocolate.

3. In a medium bowl, combine cake flour, all-purpose flour, kosher salt, cornstarch, and baking soda. Whisk together.

4. Using a stand mixer with the whisk attachment, combine brown sugar and granulated sugar. Slowly stream in the melted butter while whisking.

5. Add in the eggs one at a time, followed by the egg yolks.

6. Switch to the paddle attachment and mix in the flour mixture until smooth.

7. Fold in chopped walnuts and chocolate until well combined.

8. Transfer the dough to a bowl, cover, and refrigerate for at least 45 minutes.

9. Weigh out 120g dough balls and place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

10. Bake at 220°C for 10–13 minutes.

11. Cool on a wire rack before serving.
