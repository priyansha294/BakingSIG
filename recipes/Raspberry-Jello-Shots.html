Raspberry Jello Shots

A staple at any party hosted by me.

Author: Priyansha Ranjan

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Weikfield Raspberry Jelly Crystals
  • Absolut Raspberry Vodka
  • Regular Vodka (to supplement if needed)

Steps

1. Boil 3 glasses of water (using the iconic ISB-issued glasses for authenticity).

2. Once boiling, add the jelly mix from the Raspberry Jelly Crystals packet.

3. Stir thoroughly until fully dissolved, then add 1 glass of vodka.

4. Pour into small shot cups and refrigerate for 4â€“5 hours until set.
