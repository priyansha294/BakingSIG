Strawberry White Chocolate Cookies

We did a cookie and milk party and had fun.

Author: Shruti Priya

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups + 2 tbsp flour
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2–3/4 cup chopped strawberries
  • 1/2 cup white chocolate chips or chunks

Steps

1. Combine the butter with the white and brown sugar until creamy.

2. Add the egg and vanilla extract and mix well.

3. Add in the flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until just combined.

4. Fold in the white chocolate chips.

5. Add the chopped strawberries and lemon juice to the dough and gently mix.

6. Scoop out dough balls and place them on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

7. Bake at 180°C for 10–15 minutes depending on cookie size and oven (mine took 15 minutes).
