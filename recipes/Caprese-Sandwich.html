Caprese Sandwich

This was made during the R&D session for sandwiches to be finally made during the movie night.

Author: Priyansha Ranjan

Ingredients

  • Focaccia bread (sliced in half)
  • Arugula
  • 1 ball fresh Mozzarella cheese
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Pesto Sauce
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic

Steps

1. Roast the cherry tomatoes in a baking dish along with some peeled garlic cloves (4â€“5) and olive oil for 10 minutes.

2. Toast the bread slightly and layer one side with some pesto sauce.

3. Assemble the sandwich with arugula leaves, roasted cherry tomatoes, and fresh mozzarella shreds. Drizzle salt, pepper, and balsamic vinegar to taste.
