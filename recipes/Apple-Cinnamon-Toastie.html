Apple Cinnamon Toastie

Apple cinnamon is a staple recipe for me, and I tried to give it a twist for the Movie Night. The result was a warm and hearty dessert.

Author: Divyashree Salvi

Ingredients

  • 2 apples
  • 3 tbsps butter
  • 3 tbsps cream cheese
  • 4 slices of milk bread
  • 1/2 tbsp of chopped walnuts
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (or to taste)
  • 3 tbsp sugar (or to taste)

Steps

1. Melt 1 tbsp butter in a pan over medium heat. Add the sliced apples, cinnamon, and 1 tsp sugar. Cook until the apples are soft and caramelized, about 5 minutes. Set aside.

2. In a separate pan over low heat, toast the walnuts with honey for about 2 minutes, stirring occasionally until fragrant and slightly caramelized. Let cool.

3. In a bowl, whip the cream cheese with the remaining sugar until smooth. Fold in the toasted walnuts.

4. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly onto two slices of bread. Layer the cooked apples on top and cover with the remaining bread slices.

5. Melt the remaining 1 tbsp butter in a pan over medium heat. Toast the sandwiches until golden brown and crispy, about 2â€“3 minutes per side.
