Yoghurt Dip

My go-to dish for birthday parties and gatherings. Best enjoyed with blue Lays.

Author: Shruti Priya

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 400 gms Yoghurt
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Basil

Steps

Mix all the ingredients together in a cute bowl and voilaaaaaa.
