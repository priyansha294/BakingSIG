Lemon Sorbet

It was the palate cleanser as a part of a seven-course tasting dinner I hosted, served right after the main course.

Author: Priyansha Ranjan

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice (I used Keya's lemon juice, but freshly squeezed works great too)

Steps

1. Boil the water and sugar in a saucepan. Let it simmer for 5 minutes on medium heat and then cool for a while.

2. Add the lemon juice to the cooled mixture and stir well.

3. Pour the mixture into a flat pan (the layer should not be very thick so it freezes faster).

4. Stir the mixture every 30 minutes for 2–3 hours, then let it rest overnight. Stirring adds air and makes the sorbet lighter — make sure to turn the mixture well each time.

Notes: I didn’t use an ice cream maker. You can also add lemon peel while boiling the water-sugar mix or a bit of lemon zest at the end.
