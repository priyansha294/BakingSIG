Lemon Crinkle Cookies

I made this at the very first Baking SIG bake sale, even before we were officially an SIG. What followed was a whirlwind sugar-filled year!

Author: Divyashree Salvi

Ingredients

For the Cookie

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda

For the Coating

  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 175°C.

2. Cream together the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy (a hand mixer works great).

3. Whip in the vanilla extract, egg, lemon zest, and lemon juice.

4. Slowly add in the flour, salt, baking soda, and baking powder, mixing just until combined.

5. Measure out teaspoons of dough and freeze them for at least 1 hour (ideally 3 hours).

6. Place granulated sugar on one plate and powdered sugar on another. Roll each dough ball in granulated sugar first, then in powdered sugar.

7. Bake for 9–11 minutes or until the bottoms are just starting to brown.

8. Cool on the tray for 3 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
