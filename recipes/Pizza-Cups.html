Pizza Cups

Made 70 of these bad boys for Bandhan Bake Sale 2.0.

Author: Priyansha Ranjan

Ingredients

  • Milk Bread
  • Veggies of your choice (Corn, tomatoes, capsicum, mushroom)
  • Diced mozzarella cheese
  • Pizza sauce (store bought or homemade)

Steps

1. Cut the milk bread into circles and place them inside a muffin tray.

2. Layer the cheese, sauce and vegetables.

3. Bake in the oven at 170 degrees for 10 minutes until the cups become golden.
