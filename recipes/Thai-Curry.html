Thai Curry

Made some people cry with the spice in it.

Author: Prachi Ghalsasi

Ingredients

Paste

  • 10 soaked red chillies
  • 10 Thai bird chillies
  • 1 onion
  • Ginger
  • Lemongrass
  • Lemon zest
  • Pepper
  • Coriander seeds
  • 3 green chillies
  • Coriander stems

Curry

  • Meat and veggies of choice
  • Coconut milk
  • Fish sauce or Soy sauce

Steps

1. Blend all ingredients for the paste into a smooth paste.

2. Heat oil in a pan and add 2â€“3 tablespoons of the paste. Fry it until fragrant.

3. Add meat and cook until almost done.

4. Add vegetables and cook them through.

5. Pour in coconut milk and let the curry simmer gently.

6. Add soy sauce or fish sauce to taste and finish cooking. Serve hot!
