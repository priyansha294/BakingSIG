Eggless Pancakes

Needed something to fight 3AM junk food cravings, this recipe carried me through core terms.

Author: Priyansha Ranjan

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Milk (as needed)
  • Butter
  • Fruits of your choice

Steps

1. Mash the ripe banana with the help of a fork and add the sugar. Mix until the sugar dissolves in the banana.

2. Add the flour and mix in milk until batter reaches a runny consistency.

3. Heat the pan and apply some butter. Spread the batter and flip the pancake once the sides look done.

4. Top with pancake syrup or fruits of your choice.
