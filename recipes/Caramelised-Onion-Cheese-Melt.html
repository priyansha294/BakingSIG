Caramelised Onion Cheese Melt

This is a comfort recipe I have made a few times at ISB. It is very quick, delicious, and ideal for cold days.

Author: Divyashree Salvi

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsps butter
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

1. Thinly slice the onion and cook it on medium heat in butter until caramelised. Add sugar while cooking and season with salt and pepper.

2. Assemble the sandwiches by placing cheese and the caramelised onions between bread slices.

3. Toast the sandwiches in butter until golden brown.

Tip: I have made this with processed cheese. Any melty cheese should work well.
