Tiramisu

Experience the delightful layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream in this classic Italian dessert.

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 8 ounces mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

In a medium saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well blended. Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils. Boil gently for 1 minute, then remove from heat and allow to cool slightly. Cover tightly and chill in refrigerator for 1 hour.

In a medium bowl, beat cream with vanilla until stiff peaks form.

Whisk mascarpone into yolk mixture until smooth.

In a small shallow dish, combine coffee and 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder.

Dip each ladyfinger into coffee mixture briefly, then arrange 12 ladyfingers in the bottom of a 7x11 inch dish.

Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers, then half of the whipped cream over that.

Repeat layers and sprinkle with remaining cocoa. Cover and refrigerate for 4 to 6 hours, until set.

Chef's Tips

For a more intense coffee flavor, consider adding a tablespoon of coffee liqueur to the coffee mixture. Additionally, ensure that the mascarpone cheese is at room temperature before mixing for a smoother consistency.
